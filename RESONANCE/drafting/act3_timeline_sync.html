SYNC ISSUES IDENTIFIED

  • 32.1-32.2 SEQUENCE: Elena shoots revolver from Ash's hand BEFORE Standard crashes in? Or after? Current threads have Standard crashing first, then Elena arriving to find Ash with revolver. Need to clarify: Does Elena arrive BEFORE the crash, confront Ash, get beaten, THEN Standard crashes in to save her? Or does Standard crash first?
  • Elena vs Ash TIMING: In Elena's thread, she arrives, shoots revolver from hand, then fights him, then he's choking her when Standard crashes in. In Standard's thread, Standard crashes in while "Elena is on the floor. Ash above her. His hands on her throat." These match.
  • Hendricks' fall timing: He goes out window in 31.4, wakes in 32.0. How much time passes? Minutes? The intercut suggests simultaneous action but climbing three stories while bleeding takes time.
  • High Noon positioning: Hendricks comes through the shattered window. But the window was shattered by Standard's crash. So he climbs up to an already-broken window. This works.
  • Four's transmission: She starts transmitting in 32.1, reaches the brain, starts the ram. Is she still physically in the throne room while her consciousness is at Alpha Centauri? Yes - split consciousness. Clear this in prose.

KEY TIMING FIX

Kael scene happens BEFORE assault - he wouldn't visit a prisoner during an active attack. His overconfidence during a quiet moment creates Standard's opening. The assault starting moments later provides cover for her to move through the compound.

FOUR'S DEATH

Four dies via relay sequence + ramming Jupiter. The relay costs her pieces (doesn't know which). The medical monitor moment is her moral weight. The Deliverance is triumph ("bigger cockpit"). The impact isn't described - she's already gone. She arrives at Alpha Centauri fragmented, holds as long as she can, then Standard catches/absorbs her. Four is GONE as herself - but part of the door. Hendricks goes to the cliff alone because Four chose the bigger cockpit instead.
