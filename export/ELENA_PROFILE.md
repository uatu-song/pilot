## II. PSYCHOLOGICAL ENGINE

### The Terror

> **The Burden of Translation.** She is the only person who speaks both "Ash" (Silence) and "Sky" (Connection). She knows both sides are right, and both are wrong. She fears she will have to choose which half of herself to kill.

### The Need

> To find a "Third Way." She needs proof that Synthesis is possible without the violation of the Drop or the lobotomy of the Quiet Zone.

### The Vice

> **Dissociative Competence.** She code-switches so effortlessly that she loses track of her own voice. She treats her own trauma as "mission parameters."

### The Mutual Cure

> **Standard.** Standard is the Third Way incarnate—a machine that wants to be human, a silence that isn't empty. Standard proves Elena isn't crazy.
